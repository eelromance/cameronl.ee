Why does your website look like that?

I'm glad you asked.

The most rewarding thing about building a website from scratch is the freedom to create the rules.

This version of my site is a love letter to the wild west of the early internet, when it was up to everyone to define their own rules and imagine their own virtual worlds. I wanted to champion that bygone era and revive its optimism by embedding my site with intentionally unconventional choices that reflect my personality while also staying usable and coherent.

We have the power to create the world we want to live in, and I believe the internet should be a fun place to express one's self, don't you?

Like myself, this website continues to evolve.
