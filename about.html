Why does your website look like that?

I'm glad you asked.

The most rewarding thing about building a website from scratch is the freedom to create the rules.

I suppose I'm tired of the way today's online spaces feel. We live inside faceless corporate marketplaces, sleek luxury showrooms, and infinite attention farms. This version of my site is a love letter to the internet before the sludge, when individuals had greater control to define their own style and collectively imagine what the web could be.

In the spirit of Web 1.0, I had fun making unorthodox design choices that reflect my personality and imagination.

We have the power to create the world we want to live in, and I believe the internet should be a fun place to express one's self, don't you?
